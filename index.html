How we work

Four stages, roughly six weeks, no surprises.

01 Discover We dig into your audience, competitors and the story only your brand can tell — then write it down so we agree before a single pixel moves.
02 Sketch Rough directions across design, illustration and motion, shown early so we can kill the wrong ideas cheaply.
03 Refine The chosen direction gets built out into a full system — typography, color, illustration style and motion principles.
04 Deliver Final files, source assets and a short guide so your team can keep using everything correctly after we're gone.
